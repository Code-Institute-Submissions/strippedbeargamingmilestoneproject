Game News/Updates

Destiny 2 Season of the Drifter

Power cap increase...
The power cap will be raised to 700 within the season of the drifter. This will then be raised to 750 during the season of opulence. Time to get grinding guardians!

Gambit is getting private matches...
Players will gain access to gambit private matches, however this will only include the old maps. As the new maps are currently only available through public games/online matchmaking.

Another unknown Exotic quest is coming...
There will be an unknown exotic quest release in April.

Four new Exotics (1 weapon, 3 armour pieces)...
Arbalest - a kinetic linear fusion rifle (main perk is: Compounding Force - Fires slugs that cause massivedamage to elemental shields of enemy combatants)

Getaway Artist - Warlock gauntlets (main perk is: Dynamic Duo - Hold to convert your Arc Grenade into a supercharged Arc Soul. The Arc Soul functions as an autonomous turret.)

Stronghold - Titan gauntlets (main perk is: Clenched Fist - Guarding with Swords increases move speed and does not drain ammo. Shots blocked immediately after guarding will heal you.)

Liar's Handshake - Hunter gauntlets (main perk is: Cross Counter - Using your Arc melee ability or being hit by a melee attack will allow you to follow up with an extremely powerful melee counterpunch that will heal you.)

New Crucible, Vanguard, and Gambit pinnacle weapons...
Crucible - The Recluse (a submachine gun, it’s unique perk is: Master of Arms - Kills with any weapon improve this weapon's damage for a short time.)

Vanguard pinnacle gun - Oxygen SR3 (a scout rifle, it’s unique perk is: Meganeura - Dragonfly deals more damage based on the number of precision hits dealt to the target beforehand.)

Gambit pinnacle gun - 21% Delirium (a heavy machine gun, it’s unique perk is: Killing Tally - Kills increase this weapon's damage until it is stowed or reloaded.)

New events in May...
2 new ones, and one returning event: 1)The Revelry 2)Arc week 3)Iron Banner

Xur will sell Forsaken Exotics in the Season of the Drifter...
He will have a chance of selling forsaken exotics as one of the 4 (1 weapon/3 armour) exotics he has for sale each week. However, the fated engrams he sells each week, will not have forsaken exotics added into the loot pool yet.

Armor will feature random stat rolls in the Season of the Drifter...
Any armour you pick up after the start of the season of the drifter, can roll with resilience/recovery/mobility as its main attribute (whereas a certain armour piece may have been specced for mobility previously)

Information gathered from:
https://www.gamesradar.com/uk/destiny-2-season-of-the-drifter/
https://db.destinytracker.com/d2/en
